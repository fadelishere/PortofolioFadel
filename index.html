About Me

I am Fadel Naya Raflinanto — an Informatics Engineering student at Dian Nuswantoro University with a profound interest in graphic design and web design. I am accustomed to combining design aesthetics with user experience (UI/UX) to create works that are not only beautiful but also highly functional.
